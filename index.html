1.1 INTRODUCTION

CinderRPG is a modded Minecraft server that runs on a custom-built modpack of the same name. It’s a medieval fantasy survival RPG with MMO elements, developed for players who enjoy a slower-paced, content rich, progression-focused experience.

Created by lead Lucifer_WPF (also known as BigCatLuci) and co-developers DivineHerobrine and Bulldog2208 (also known as Jeff).

The modpack is currently available on the Technic Launcher, with a potential release on CurseForge planned for the future.
Visit Technic Page
After joining the server, you’ll arrive at the new player spawn. There, you’ll find several signs explaining key systems — it’s strongly recommended you take the time to read them, as they cover mechanics you’ll need to be familiar with.

To begin your journey and leave spawn, you must first interact with the Remembrance Stone. This will prompt you to choose from one of eight playable races. If you’d like to learn more about any of the options before deciding, you can press the button with that race’s name to open a description screen.

Each race will belong to one of four factions, which you can see boldly above their icons. Lore wise this indicates the religion & allegiances of said race.

Each race comes with its own unique lore and backstory, as well as a distinct set of base classes and subclasses. These are the core of what defines a race in gameplay terms — think of them as your starting class. They determine your initial role, abilities, and where your character has their early stat points allocated.

For example, choosing the Lich race will assign you the Sorcerer base class, along with the Conjuration and Destruction subclasses. This makes it a strong choice for players who want to focus on magic, particularly destructive spells and summoning or necromancy.

There are also ‘Affinities’ very minor passives unique to each race, making them slightly more efficient in different fields then others. Often aligning with their lore.

Finally, each race will have different elected officials who run their respective race, making choices about the race and its activities, setting policies, promoting others etc. They’ll also have their own race capitals/kingdoms. This is where as a community you can come together to build, prosper, setup shops and so forth. You’ll automatically be teleported there when using the overworld portal at spawn, alternatively you can use the command ‘/race capital’.
