Simply put, there is no “best race”, each person will have their own opinions and banter on the relations between races but the choice should always be up to the user. Races only really affect the gameplay during the very start of the game; innate points can be refunded via resetting points with a Mindwipe, and players won't alienate new players for simply making a choice based on preference. The choice of a race and its weight is completely dependent on the person. Determine what you find to be a higher priority such as, its lore and appearance, its current playerbase, its potential, or its faction-and go from there. Races don't lock you out of gameplay, nor give outstanding advantages, you can associate with who you wish, and do what you wish.

Choosing a class in CinderRPG depends on your preferred playstyle. Consider whether you enjoy close combat, magic, stealth, or support roles. Each class offers unique abilities and strengths, so think about how you want to engage with the game world and other players.

Races are integral to many aspects and themes of Cinder. They cannot be changed, but a player's gameplay does not require implication with races, and can simply see them as cosmetic if they feel the need to.

Wares can be sold in multiple ways, notably signs can be attached to chests with the following format per line :
[Player Username]; [Amount per click]; B or/and S [Price]; [Item ID]
Buying (B) and Selling (S) is seen through the perspective other players.

Season 4 is currently in early Beta. There is no intention of resetting the server anytime soon for a 5th season. Considering the period of prior seasons, this season will likely last another 2 years at the very least.

The server restarts every 6 hours. Keep an eye out on the in-game message to find out what time that is relative to your time zone.

Shares are a premium currency, given with every purchase and redeemed monthly by supporter ranks. The amount of shares gained varies with the price of the purchase, with a significant boost to server-wide bonuses, such as crate key explosions.
Various items can be bought using shares in the supporter shop, located behind the guild hall.

Mods that mostly affect the QOL of the game are completely allowed, we even have a list in our discord. However, malicious mods or ones that provide unfair advantages are against the server's rules. Players found abusing advantages such as automated actions/macros, Xray, or other cheats will be punished.
