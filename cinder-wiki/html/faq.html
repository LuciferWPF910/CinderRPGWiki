Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about CinderRPG

Simply put, there is no “best race”, each person will have their own opinions and banter on the relations between races but the choice should always be up to the user. Races only really affect the gameplay during the very start of the game; innate points can be refunded via resetting points with a Mindwipe, and players won't alienate new players for simply making a choice based on preference. The choice of a race and its weight is completely dependent on the person. Determine what you find to be a higher priority such as, its lore and appearance, its current playerbase, its potential, or its faction-and go from there. Races don't lock you out of gameplay, nor give outstanding advantages, you can associate with who you wish, and do what you wish.

Choosing a class in CinderRPG depends on your preferred playstyle. Consider whether you enjoy close combat, magic, stealth, or support roles. Each class offers unique abilities and strengths, so think about how you want to engage with the game world and other players.

Races are integral to many aspects and themes of Cinder. They cannot be changed, but a player's gameplay does not require implication with races, and can simply see them as cosmetic if they feel the need to.

You can reset your skills, including initial points added by your race, by using a Mindwipe Tonic. The first one can be obtained in the AllHollows Crypts Dungeon, in shard 1.

To create a guild simply do /guild create (name), and invite your friends with /guild invite (name).

If you're looking for a guild then why not ask around? Players are always looking for more members to bolster their numbers with. Head on over to the #guild-recruitement channel, in the discord, and have a look for current listings or advertise yourself with your own recruitment post.

Happy hunting!

Yes, you can play with friends from other races. While races have unique traits and abilities, they do not restrict party formation or collaboration in gameplay.

Generally it's always safe to pick classes depending on your current situation, or simply based on personal preference. If you're playing with friends that intend on spending points in combat classes, then why not buff them with Apothecary potions, provide cover fire with destruction magic or arrows, or gather resources and funds with better efficacy via the Treasure Hunter, Collector, and Thief classes?

CinderRPG's server does not have support for cracked clients due to its usage of the minecraft authentication servers. An official version of minecraft java edition is required in order to experience the game as, whilst playable, singleplayer mode does not offer the full experience.

If your question isn't answered or you feel that it wasn't specific enough, join us on the BigHatStudios Discord. Members of the community can provide you with much more detailed answers, and offer you advice as you progress through your playthrough.

To report a bug, please use the bug report form or reach out to the staff team via the BHS Discord.

A "Backpack" in cinder is known as a Witch's Pouch. These are essentially separate Ender Chests awarded upon completion of very specific quests within the shard worlds, or purchased via Shares (See Shop).

Season 4 is currently in early Beta. There is no intention of resetting the server anytime soon for a 5th season. Considering the period of prior seasons, this season will likely last another 2 years at the very least.

Mods that mostly affect the QOL of the game are completely allowed, we even have a list in our discord. However, malicious mods or ones that provide unfair advantages are against the server's rules. Players found abusing mods that grant them advantages such as automated actions/macros, Xray, or other cheats will be punished.
