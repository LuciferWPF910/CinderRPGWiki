Shares System

TL;DR

Quick overview for the hasty adventurer.

  • What are Shares? A premium currency earned through real-world support, redeemable for unique in-game items, missions, cosmetics, mounts, and privileges.
  • How to Get Them: Purchasing packages from our store. Ranks offer monthly shares.
  • Key Benefits: Rewards your continued patronage and especially rewards charitable users. Players can purchase things with a real value (such as crate keys) with shares.
  • Pro Tip: Buying things for others in the form as a gift rewards more shares, however! purchasing things that benefits everyone, such as crate explosions net the most shares!

In-Depth Guide

Delve deeper into the Shares system, here is an in depth and detailed explaination of shares and how they work...

• The Basics of Shares

Shares an exclusive currency, they currently can only be earnt via patronage on our store. Either as a bonus purchase reward or a monthly reward attached to a rank. We designed shares to be our way of giving something back to those who support the CinderRPG project and its continual development, for this reason you will receive a greater number of shares for purchasing packages for others (in the form of a gift) and an even greater sum of shares for purchasing packages that benefit the entire community, for example Crate Key Explosions.

Shares cannot be farmed through quests or looted from dungeons. They are acquired exclusively through our Patronage Store, different purchases will offer different quantities of shares, the exact amount will be specified in the details of the package under 'shares', you should note that purchases made for others in the form of a gift will reward you with more shares then a purchase for yourself. Additionally, packages that benefit everyone- such as crate key explosions offer the largest amount of shares (by price).
