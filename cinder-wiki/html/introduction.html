Enter a Shattered Realm

'You have returned to the waking world ashen, before you lie the remains of a shattered reality, crumbling beneath the weight of a tyrant who falsely thinks himself a God. Perhaps this is the purpose of your return- or perhaps not. But one thing remains clear; your slate is clean, it lies solely within your hands to decide your face, what you will do? who will you become? Coward or killer? Warrior or Scholar? Hero or Villian?'

CinderRPG is the passion project of a small but dedicated team, inspired by a multitude of games & genres, and packed into a content rich survival RPG, aimed at providing multiple ways to experience the server.

Join 1 of 8 races, level up your classes, acquire unique gear, find new friends to conquer dungeons as a guild, subdue opposing races in grand wars, and add your undertakings to the history & lore of the server.

A unique medieval fantasy

Explore a magical world, brimming with hand built structures, custom creature and weapon models, an original soundtrack, and intricate lore seeping throughout every choice, and detail.

CinderRPG takes inspiration from a plethora of different genres, interpreted and adapted in a manner to add onto an ever-expanding world

Every build, model, and soundtrack is made by a member of the community and the committed staff team. All with the intent to properly project a unique aesthetic via its themes and details.

Strive

Reach higher

Be it your choice of involvement, each player starts as 1 of 8 races.

Our Values

Yet it isn’t simply about running & playing the server.
Year after year, BigHat Studios and its community have shown their commitment to combatting truly foul diseases through its yearly- Charity Event.

Each year a month is dedicated to fund raising for a good cause, providing players an incentive via in-game benefits and bonuses, while helping to fund research against diseases such as Cancer or Supporting children battling cancer, supporting specialist hospitals for children and more.
