Commands Reference

Master the arcane syntax of CinderRPG

Integral Commands

/discord
Get the link to the official CinderRPG Discord server
/help
Display the main help menu with available commands
/race help
View detailed information about all available races
/kits
List all available starter kits and rank kits
/kit (kit name)
Claim a specific kit by name
/back
Return to your previous location after teleporting

RPG Commands

/shard (number)
Travel to a specific shard world (1, 2, or 3)
/world
Return to the main overworld hub
/enchant
Open the enchantment interface for your held item
/refine
Upgrade your equipment using refinement materials
/class
Open your class skill tree and progression menu
/class display
Toggle the display of your current class in chat
/profile
View your character profile, stats, and achievements

Overworld Commands

/sethome (name)
Set a named home location at your current position
/home (name)
Teleport to one of your saved home locations
/trust (username)
Grant another player full access to your claim
/trustlist
View all players trusted in your current claim
/accesstrust (username)
Give limited interaction permissions in your claim
/containertrust (username)
Allow container access within your claim
/permissiontrust (username)
Let another player manage trust in your claim
/claimlist
Display all your claims and their locations
/subdivideclaim
Create a subdivision within an existing claim
/abandonclaim
Delete the claim you're currently standing in
/abandonallclaims
Remove all of your claims (use with caution!)

Rank Commands

Many rank commands scale with tier. Higher ranks unlock more features, better rewards, and additional customization options like colored text and prefixes.

Slayer

/enderchest /ec Access your ender chest from anywhere
/nickname /nick Set a custom display name
/top Teleport to the highest block at your location
/buyclaimblocks Buy extra claim blocks
/transferclaimblocks Transfer claim blocks between players
/disposal Open a disposal interface to delete items
/rtp Randomly teleport to a safe location
/redeem Claim rank-specific rewards and bonuses

Undead Slayer

Includes all Slayer commands
/craft Open a portable crafting table
/nickname /nick Set a custom display name with colored text [View colors]

Demon Slayer

Includes all Undead Slayer commands
/feed Instantly restore your hunger bar
/repair Repair the item in your hand

Soul Slayer / Custom

Includes all Demon Slayer commands
/repairall Repair all items in your inventory
/heal Instantly restore your health
/cinderprefix Set a custom colored prefix before your name
